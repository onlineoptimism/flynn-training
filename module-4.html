Module 7

Final Project

Time to build something real. Pick a project, use the tools you've learned, and show the team what you made. This is the fun part.

Pick Your Project

Choose one of the three options below. There's no wrong choice — pick whatever feels most useful to your actual work.

Option A

Department Prompt Pack

Beginner

Build a collection of 5 high-quality prompts specific to your department and role. Test each one in Claude Cowork, refine until they consistently produce great output, and document them in a shared Google Doc your team can use.

Tools: Claude Cowork

Option B

AI-Assisted Client Deliverable

Intermediate

Use Claude Chat or Cowork to help produce a real client deliverable — a blog post, social campaign, ad copy set, or report. Document your prompt process and include a before/after showing your edits. Submit both the output and a quick write-up of what worked.

Tools: Claude Chat or Cowork

Option C

Build + Deploy a Web Page

Advanced

Use Claude Code to build a simple HTML page — a client landing page, an internal tool, or a personal portfolio. Deploy it to a subdomain following the steps from Module 6, and share the live URL. Bonus points for a working CLAUDE.md in the project folder.

Tools: Claude Code + Cloudflare Pages

How to Submit

  1. Complete your chosen project. Quality over speed — this is a real deliverable, not a quiz.
  2. If it's a document, save it to your department's Google Drive folder.
  3. If it's a web page, make sure it's live at your OO subdomain URL.
  4. Copy the Slack template below, fill it in, and post in #ai-and-automations.
  5. Your team lead will acknowledge your post. That's it — you're officially certified. 🎓
