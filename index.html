From chat to code — let's get you up to speed.

This course covers the three Claude tools we use at OO: Claude Chat, Claude Cowork, and Claude Code. By the end, you'll know which one to reach for, how to use it for your specific role, and you'll have built and deployed something real. 🎉

  • A clear mental model for when to use each tool (and when NOT to)
  • Walkthroughs specific to your department — Content, Social, Paid Ads, Creative, Web, New Business, Growth, Video, and AE
  • Step-by-step instructions for deploying a live website to Cloudflare Pages
  • A finished project on your own OO subdomain that you can share with the team
